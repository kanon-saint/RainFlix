How to Play a Movie or Series

  1. Search for the movie or series in IMDb or The Movie Database.
  2. Copy the ID from the URL.
    • IMDb example: tt15398776
    • TMDb example: 1399
  3. Choose Movie Player or Series Player.
  4. For a series, enter the season and episode number. Example: Season 1, Episode 1.
  5. Use the built-in catalogs below to load sample movies and shows faster.

Note: Catalog items are editable in the code. Custom catalog items added through the form are saved in this browser only.
